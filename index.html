Bienvenue

As a physicist by nature, I always try to find the "Question" and the "Answer", mostly at the same time. If I get the question, I barely have the answer. Somtime with the answer in hand, I even forget the question. Honestly, my primary job is to think about some problem, and then in order to solve that, I write code, and eventually start finding bugs in it. Sometimes I manage to fix a few bugs and then life becomes happy again, even at the end without knowing the "Answer" that I was trying to find ... :P

Caught in this endless loop, I arrived in Strasbourg as a postdoc at CMS. Although, I have been diving in the same process since 2017, but keeping "Life is beautiful" in mind, I invite you to this "Loopy" world of mine.
